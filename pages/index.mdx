## **What is Semaphor?**

**Semaphor** is a state-of-the-art integrated analytics solution designed to seamlessly blend with your apps, providing real-time data insights directly to your users. With this approach, you can deliver insights and analytics capabilities to your end users and decision-makers right through your app itself, eliminating the need for them to learn and use a separate BI tool.

Semaphor offers a suite of analytics experiences, including customizable dashboards, clean rooms, and natural-language powered analytics, all designed to be integrated within your existing software applications. Whether you're looking to enhance a SaaS product, or an internal business application, Semaphor provides the capabilities you need to turn your data into actionable insights.
